About Monroe

Dr. Monroe Mann, PhD, Esq, MBA, LLM, ME is an attorney, actor, musician, singer, public speaker, book author, teacher, coach, linguist, consultant, web designer, entrepreneur, and a bronze-star nominated Iraq war veteran.

Some highlights include:

  • PhD in psychology from Capella University, started 2011 and earned in 2016, conducting original research on the decision-making processes of successful entertainers
  • One-time Ostrich riding champion in South Africa
  • Trained -32 degree Fahrenheit dogsledder through Outward Bound in Minnesota
  • General class amateur radio operator
  • One of two lead producers on You Can't Kill Stephen King, as well as co-director, co-writer, and co-star
